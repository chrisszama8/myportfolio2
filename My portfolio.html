// About Me

Crafting Visual Stories
That Inspire Action

My name is Danny Symon, a passionate Graphic Designer based in Lilongwe, Malawi. I have been working in the creative industry since 2016, helping brands, artists, and businesses communicate their ideas through strong and impactful visual design.

I specialize in creating high-quality graphics for branding, social media, marketing, and digital campaigns. Over the years, I have developed strong skills in Adobe Photoshop, as well as experience using Adobe Illustrator and CorelDRAW to deliver professional and creative design solutions.

Currently, I work as a Graphic Designer at Hustle Media Production, where I create visual content for brands and businesses across different industries. My goal is always to combine creativity with strategy to produce designs that not only look good but also communicate effectively.

Beyond my professional work, I continue to explore new ideas, trends, and creative techniques to keep improving my craft and delivering designs that make a lasting impression.

// Case Study

Branding &
Visual Identity

Zifika Express

Delivery Service Branding

This branding project was created for Zifika Express, a delivery and errand service brand focused on speed, reliability, and convenience. The logo creatively combines the letter "Z" with a delivery van and motion lines to visually communicate fast delivery and efficient service.

The green color represents growth, trust, freshness, and reliability, while the bold typography gives the brand a strong and modern identity. The overall design reflects a professional doorstep delivery service that is easy to recognize and memorable.

The complete brand identity system includes applications across tote bags, mugs, caps, water bottles, notebooks, pens, and coasters, demonstrating versatility and consistency across all touchpoints.
